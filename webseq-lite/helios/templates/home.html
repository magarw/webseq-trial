Welcome to WebSeq Lite!

We have developed several modules to support customized analyses of human genetic data. Please choose one of the modules below to begin your custom analysis.

WebSeq is built by researchers, for researchers, and is not intended for clinical diagnostic use.

This module will help you conduct a family-based analysis from start to finish. Starting with CSV files from the VCF to Annovar CSV module, you can clean and prefilter the data based on the types of variants you’re interested in, add additional annotation, filter by allele frequency, and finally filter by mode of inheritance.

This module supports cohort-based analyses. Starting with CSV files from the VCF to Annovar CSV module, you can: Detect genetic homogeneity in your cohort, search for all variants in a particular gene in your cohort, remove false positives from your cohort analysis results, and visualize pathways.


This module allows you to upload a csv file (e.g. of candidate variants resulting from a Family-Based Analysis, or a Cohort-Based Analysis) and automates the visual inspection of the reads in IGV. For this module, you must have the BAM and BAI files for each individual in the analysis.

This module allows you to easily extract variants in genes of interest (from a user-defined list) from an individual’s CSV file. For example, this module may be used after the Allele Frequency filter, or after the Modes of Inheritance filter.
